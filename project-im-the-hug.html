I'm the hug is Mathilde Luijten, Abel Ghekiere, Simon Mitxelena and Orlan Ghekiere. Each with their own background, the musicians bring singer-songwriter, folk, indie pop and jazz together into an intimate sound. For fans of Hayley Heynderickx, Florist and Keaton Henson.
